How to bake a classic chocolate cake

ingredients you need

  • flour
  • cocoa powder
  • leavening agents(baking powder & baking soda)
  • vanila extract
  • sugar
  • egg
  • milk
  • oil
  • boiling hot water- yes you readed it right!
    you need it to make the cake batter
  • unsalted butter

step by step instructions

  1. preheat the oven to 175c or 350f for at least 15mins.
    grease and line 2-6 inch cake pans. you can also make a single layer cake
    in a 7 or 8 inch pan. you can also just grease and dust with some flour or cocoa.
  2. place a sieve over a mixind bowl add flour.
  3. add the rest of the dry ingredients- cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. sieve them all.
  4. next add sugar
  5. mix everything well until uniform. make a well in the center
  6. heat half cup water in a pot. (don't add the water here).
    meanwhile pour milk, egg, oil and vanila to the dry ingredients.
  7. whisk the wet ingredients frist. then whisk together wet and dry ingredients
    just until combined. do not over mix
  8. you will have a slightly thick batter at this stage.
  9. quickly pour the hot water to the batter. the water must be very hot
  10. immediately whisk the batter just until the water is absorbed and the batter
    looks uniform. batter is very runny at this stage
  11. pour this to the pans. tap the pan to the counter 2 to 3 times.
  12. bake for 28 to 30mins or until the tester comes out clean. please adjust the timing depending
    on the oven and pan sizes. if baking in a 7 inch pan you may need to bake up to 35 to 38mins. cool the pans on a wire rack for 10mins
  13. then invert to a cooling rack. cool completly before frosting

    14. frosting chocolate cake

  14. spread a tsp of frosting in the center of the cake board or cake stand. place 4 parchment
    papers on all 4 sides such that they don't touch the frosting. place the cake on the parchment pap[er. the center of the
    cake has to be on the frosting that was spread. begin to spread the frosting with a spatula. even out for smooth surface. place
    another cake and repeat the frosting. then frost on the sides. smoothen the sides as well with the spatula.
  15. i just used choco chips and sprinkles on the top. then remove the parchment paper gently
  16. all done! now you may slice the classic chocolate cake.
